About Me

👋 Hi,

I am Ansar Ibrahim, a passionate and dedicated Maldivian footballer, driven by my love for the game and my desire to represent my country at the highest level. Since my early years, football has been more than just a sport to me; it's been a way of life. Over the course of my career, I have had the privilege of playing for various clubs in the Maldivian league, where I have honed my skills and developed into a versatile and reliable player.

Throughout my journey, I have also had the honor of wearing the national jersey, representing the Maldives in international competitions. With every match, I strive to push my limits, aiming to contribute not just to my team's success but to the growing football scene in our country.

Off the field, I believe in giving back to the community by inspiring the next generation of athletes. Whether through youth coaching or participating in local initiatives, I aim to use football as a tool to bring people together and promote a healthy, active lifestyle.
